What: Capture The Flag (CTF)

Cybersecurity challenges, commonly known as **Capture The Flag (CTF)** competitions, are a type of security exercise where participants solve a variety of technical problems to find a secret string—the "flag." These challenges cover almost every domain in cybersecurity, from reverse engineering and forensics to web exploitation and cryptography. They serve as an excellent, legal, and educational environment for aspiring hackers and security professionals to sharpen their skills.

Primary CTF Categories

CategoryDescription
**Web Exploitation**Finding vulnerabilities in web applications (SQLi, XSS).
**Forensics**Analyzing disk images or network traffic to recover flags.
**Cryptography**Breaking or analyzing encryption algorithms.
**Reverse Engineering**Analyzing compiled binary code to understand its logic.
